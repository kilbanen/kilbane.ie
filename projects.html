Scrabble Anagram Solver

This is a C program that I wrote to determine every word that can be made from a set of Scrabble tiles. It even incorporates blank tiles! Of course, there is absolutely no way you would be allowed to use this in a real game of Scrabble, so this is just for your own curiosity. You could also use it for the words rounds on Countdown, or finding anagrams of your own name.

Connect 4

This is an implementation of Connect 4 written in ARM assembly language that I wrote for an assignment in my first year of college. You can play against another person, or against my extremely primitive AI.
